RIVERS

Thunder Rapids

The Thunder Rapids is a thrilling stretch of whitewater river that winds through the rugged Colorado Mountains. Known for its rapid currents and jagged rocks, it offers an adrenaline-pumping experience for adventurous rafters. Wildlife such as eagles and river otters can often be spotted along the banks, adding to the natural beauty of the journey.

Crystal Creek

Crystal Creek is a calm yet enchanting river, famous for its sparkling, clear waters that reflect the surrounding desert landscape. Ideal for families and beginners, this river features gentle rapids and plenty of quiet stretches perfect for relaxation. Little Colorado

The Little Colorado River is a legendary tributary of the mighty Colorado River, carving its way through dramatic canyons and colorful cliffs. With a mix of moderate rapids and peaceful pools, it offers both challenge and tranquility. Ancient Native American sites and fascinating geological formations line the river, providing a once-in-a-lifetime rafting experience.
